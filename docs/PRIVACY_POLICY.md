# Privacy Policy for DeepBrief

**Last Updated:** January 3, 2026

DeepBrief ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension handles your data.

## 1. Data Collection
We do **not** collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII).

We only process the specific text you highlight and choose to explain. When you use the "Explain this..." feature:
*   The selected text is sent securely via HTTPS to our backend server (hosted on Google Cloud).
*   Our backend processes this text using the Gemini 2.5 AI API to generate an explanation.
*   The explanation is returned to your browser.

## 2. Data Usage
The data (your selected text) is used **solely** for the purpose of generating the explanation you requested.
*   We do **not** log or save your selected text after the request is completed.
*   We do **not** track your browsing history or visited URLs.
*   We do **not** use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

## 3. Third-Party Services
*   **Google Cloud Platform:** Our backend is hosted on Google Cloud Run.
*   **Google Gemini API:** We use Google's AI service to generate explanations. The data sent to the AI model is stateless and is not used to train the model.

## 4. User Consent
By installing and using the DeepBrief extension, you consent to the processing of your selected text as described in this policy.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
